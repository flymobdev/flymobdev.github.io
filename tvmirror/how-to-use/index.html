TV Mirror - How to use

Before you start

  • Connect your phone and TV/streaming device to the same Wi‑Fi network (same router and subnet).
  • Turn on the TV and keep it on the home screen. Make sure Chromecast/TV stick is powered.
  • Turn off VPN/proxy on your phone. For smoother streaming, use a 5 GHz Wi‑Fi band.

Quick start

  1. Open TV Mirror. The app scans nearby TVs and casting devices automatically.
  2. Select your device (Chromecast, AirPlay TV/Apple TV, LG webOS TV, or Miracast‑compatible device).
  3. Choose mirroring quality: resolution and frame rate (e.g., 720p/30 fps for balance).
  4. To mirror audio, enable Sound Mirroring. On first use, grant Microphone permission.
  5. Tap Start Mirroring. Approve the Android “Screen capture” permission prompt.
  6. Your screen appears on the TV. Use the main screen or the notification toolbar to pause/stop.

Device‑specific tips

  • Chromecast/Google TV: Ensure the same Wi‑Fi. If asked to pair or enter a PIN, confirm on the TV.
  • AirPlay (Apple TV/AirPlay 2 TVs): Make sure AirPlay is enabled on the TV. Enter the on‑screen PIN if prompted.
  • LG webOS TV: Keep TV on the same network and allow connections from mobile devices. Accept pairing on first connect.
  • Miracast devices: Enable “Wireless display/Screen share” on the TV, then pick it in TV Mirror.

Controls and tweaks

  • Start/Stop/Reconnect from the main screen.
  • Quick Stop from the notification toolbar.
  • To change resolution or frame rate, stop mirroring first, adjust, then reconnect.
