Google Play summary

Cast photos and videos to a bigger screen

The Google Play listing describes TV Cast around photo casting, video casting, and companion remote controls. This page summarizes those capabilities in FlyMobile's own wording without using third-party brand assets.

Android app Photo and video casting Remote mode

Photo casting

Show single photos or small photo sets on a TV-sized display, with simple viewing controls for living-room sharing.

Video casting

Send personal videos from your phone to a compatible receiver for a larger viewing experience. Format support depends on the receiver.

Remote mode

Keep basic remote controls available while casting, so you can manage common TV actions from the same Android device.

How to start

  1. Connect your phone and receiving device to the same local network.
  2. Open TV Cast and choose the photo or video you want to view.
  3. Select a compatible receiver and start casting.

Good to know

TV Cast is intended for common wireless casting setups. Actual support depends on the receiver, network conditions, media format, and device permissions. FlyMobile is independent and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any TV or device brand.
