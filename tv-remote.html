Google Play summary

Universal TV control from your phone

The Google Play listing presents TV Remote as a universal remote experience for controlling many TVs from a mobile device. This page keeps that same focus: quick control, familiar remote actions, and convenient typing from your phone when the TV setup supports it.

Android app Remote control TV navigation

Remote controls

Use on-screen directional controls, selection, and common playback actions when your TV or streaming device is reachable on the network.

Simpler navigation

The app is organized around a familiar remote layout so everyday TV browsing does not require finding the physical remote first.

Text input

For compatible setups, phone keyboard input can make searches and short text fields easier than typing with a TV on-screen keyboard.

How to start

  1. Connect your phone and TV device to the same home network.
  2. Open TV Remote and select a compatible device.
  3. Use the on-screen controls from your phone.

Good to know

TV Remote is designed for many common smart TV and streaming-device setups. Availability can vary by device model, network, TV software, and permissions. FlyMobile is an independent app developer and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any TV or device brand.
